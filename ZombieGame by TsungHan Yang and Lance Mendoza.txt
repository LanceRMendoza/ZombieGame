// ZombieGame Project Read Me
Project Created by TsungHan Yang and Lance Mendoza

When opening the Unity Project ZombieGame the game starts in the Start 
menu.

Click on the "Start" button to start the game.

Your objective is to collect all of the blue spheres(coins) to complete the level.

Move the character via controller "W, A, S, D". 
Press the "Space Bar" button to jump on platforms and attain the blue spheres.
Press the "Left Mouse" button to fire your weapon.

Survive from the zombie horde if you can!

Have fun!
